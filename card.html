Fox

Foxes are one of the best-hearing animals. Specifically, red foxes can hear a watch ticking 36 meters away.

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Order: Carnivora
  • Family: Canidae

Foxes are small-to-medium sized and omnivoroues mammals. They belong to the Canidae family. There are 12 different species that are usually called "true" foxes, but 25 more species, current or extinct, are also called foxes sometimes.
